What We Do

Six core services to keep your property looking its best — all year.

Lawn Maintenance

Precision mowing with commercial-grade equipment, crisp edging, detailed trimming, and a thorough clean-blow every visit. We stripe your lawn like the pros.

Landscape Design

Custom garden beds, decorative borders, mulch installation, and seasonal color planting — all designed to maximize your property's curb appeal and value.

Fertilization & Weed Control

6-step lawn treatment program calibrated for Michigan soil and climate. Pre-emergent, broadleaf control, slow-release feeding — thick, green results guaranteed.

Seasonal Cleanups

Thorough spring and fall cleanups: complete leaf removal, bed prep, gutter clearing, debris haul-off, and seasonal transitions to keep your property immaculate.

Shrub & Hedge Trimming

Expert shaping and seasonal pruning for all shrub varieties. Clean lines, healthy growth, and manicured beds year-round.

Snow Removal

Reliable residential snow plowing, driveway clearing, and sidewalk salt treatment. Early-bird contracts available — never worry about a Michigan winter again.
