About Us

Our Story

Lee Bros Xscapes started with a simple idea: what if a lawn care company actually cared as much about your yard as you do? Growing up right here in Southeast Michigan, we spent our weekends helping family and neighbors keep their properties looking sharp.

Today, we're proud to serve 500+ properties across the 734 — from spring blooms to winter snow, all four seasons. Our commitment: professional results, clear communication, and genuine care for every yard we touch. Because your lawn is our reputation.
